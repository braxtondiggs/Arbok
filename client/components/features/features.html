features

Everyone's Music

Everyone can add thier music to a collective queue/list that will get played later in the Alma player. How and when the music gets played is the twist.

Be Heard

Dislike the song and watch the song move down the queue and possibly get skipped. Like the song? Your song gets saved for later and get your song to play sooner.

In-App Interactions

Connect with other users within the Alma player, share text, emoticons & even photos amongst everyone. Share that amazing selfe with everyone in the room.

Vast Catalog

Search for music videos based on your individual taste, with our extensive catalog find undiscovered artists from around the world.

Brand New Music

Not sure what to play? Not a problem, discover new/trending emerging artists and music videos across the globe.

Upcoming Concert Info

Love the artist that is playing? Instantly be notified when an artist will have a upcoming concerts. Alos, get live feeds from their social media accounts like Twitter, Facebook & Instagram.
